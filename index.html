Meet ELIZA

Record voice memos from anywhere and let ELIZA do the rest. Using IBM-Watson, ELIZA will translate your speech to text and query it to determine your mental state.

With ELIZA, you can find out more about your emotional health and see if you at risk for a mental disorder
